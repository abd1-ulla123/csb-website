Our Story

Crafted with passion, inspired by love.

Welcome to CSB (Cahaya Surya Bakti)

CSB was born out of a simple dream — to spread joy through the art of thoughtful gifting. What started as a passion for creating small handmade items for loved ones grew into a cozy corner for anyone looking to make their moments memorable.

From delicate bouquets to custom tote bags, from intricate quilled earrings to melt-in-your-mouth cookies, every CSB product tells a story — one of love, effort, and care. Each item is handcrafted or selected with a focus on quality, emotion, and that perfect personal touch.

CSB (Cahaya Surya Bakti) translates to “Light of the Rising Sun.” Just like sunlight brings warmth and brightness to every morning, CSB aims to bring smiles and warmth to every celebration, big or small.
